Hi there! I'm Martin, a passionate designer and front-end developer, currently living in wonderful Berlin, Germany.

I've successfully finished my apprenticeship as a media designer in 2013, where I first worked some more time as an full-time employee. In 2014 I decided to go freelance to work closer with start-ups and agencies. Since then I've gained a lot of experience with several coding languages (Ruby on Rails, PHP and alike) frameworks like Boostrap, AngularJS, Backbone, Ionic etc.) and even some Obj-C! Learning something new is essential in this business and as I mentioned earlier, I'm really passionate about this.

You'll find me a lot on Twitter, hanging around on Designer/Hacker News, posting shots on Dribbble and lifting weights in the gym to keep myself fit. When I got the time, I also like to contribute to open source projects and hacking on side-projects.
