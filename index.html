Hi! I'm Martin.

Designer & Developer

Willkommen! I'm Martin, a Designer & Front-end Developer with a passion for all things web. Crafting rich User Experiences with minimal and aesthetically pleasing interfaces.

After successfully completing my apprenticeship as a Media Designer in 2013, I decided to become a freelancer working with startups and agencies to gain more experience. Since then I have had the opportunity to work with several programming languages such as Javascript, PHP, Ruby, and their various frameworks.

I am always looking for new ways to improve myself and keep up to date, I love to learn new things and find it exciting to keep up with the industry.

Currently I am working with an agency called nerdgeschoss as the lead UI/UX designer & developer. In my free time you can catch me tweeting, lurking on Designer News/Hacker News and posting shots to Dribbble. I also enjoy reading good books on typography and design, and contributing to Open Source projects.
