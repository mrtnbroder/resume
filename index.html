Bonjour! I'm Martin, a Designer & Front-end Developer with a passion for all things web. Crafting beautiful and rich User Experiences with a minimal and aesthetic UI is what I love to do.

After I had successfully finished my apprenticeship as a Media-Designer in 2013, I decided to go freelance to gain more experience with start-ups and young agencys. Since then, I had the opportunity to get in touch with several coding languages like JavaScript, PHP, Ruby and frameworks alike.

I'm always open to new things and keeping myself up to date, not only because it is important in this industry, but also as it is a passion of mine.

I am currently working at an agency called nerdgeschoss as the leading UI/UX Designer & Developer. I like to tweet regulary, lurking on Designer/Hacker News, posting shots on Dribbble and keeping myself fit at the gym. I also got a strong interest in good typography/design books and contributing to Open Source projects.
