My name is Martin, and I'm a passionate designer and front-end developer, currently living in Berlin, Germany.

I've successfully finished my apprenticeship as a media designer in 2013, where I first worked some more time as an full-time employee. In 2014 I decided to go freelance to work closer with start-ups and agencies. Since then I've gained a lot of experience with several coding languages including JavaScript, Ruby, PHP and frameworks like Bootstrap, AngularJS, Backbone, Ionic, Laravel and more. I constantly try to stay open for new things and keep myself up-to-date.

You'll find me a lot on Twitter, hanging around on Designer/Hacker News, posting shots on Dribbble and lifting weights in the gym to keep myself fit. When I got the time, I also like to contribute to Open Source Projects and reading Typograhpy/Design Books.
